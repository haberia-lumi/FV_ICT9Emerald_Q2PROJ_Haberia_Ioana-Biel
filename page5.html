Sinigang

Ingredients


  • 2 lbs. pork belly
  • 1 lb. young tamarind
  • 1 bunch water spinach, chopped
  • 8 pieces string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2 pieces eggplants, sliced
  • 2 pieces long green pepper
  • 8 pieces okras
  • 2 pieces tomatoes, sliced into wedges
  • 1 piece onion, sliced into wedges
  • 2 quarts water
  • Fish sauce and ground black pepper

Directions


  • For forty minutes, bring two quarts of water to a boil with the young tamarind. Use a colander or a kitchen sieve to filter the tamarind broth. After that, squeeze the tamarind to release any leftover juices.
  • Fill a cooking saucepan with the tamarind broth. Add the pork belly, onion, and half the tomatoes after letting it boil.
  • After removing the floating scum, add 1 tablespoon of fish sauce, cover, and simmer for an additional hour.
  • Add the eggplants and daikon radish. Cook for five minutes.
  • Add the remaining tomatoes, okra, string beans, and long green pepper. Cook for three minutes.
  • After adding the chopped water spinach stalks, season with ground black pepper and fish sauce. Cook for two minutes.
  • Add the water spinach leaves. Shut off the heat and cover. Before serving, let the leaves cook for three minutes in the remaining heat.
