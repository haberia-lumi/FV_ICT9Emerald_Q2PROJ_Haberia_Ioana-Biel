Cheese Stick

Ingredients


  • 7 ounces cheddar cheese, sliced into individual strips
  • 30 pieces spring roll
  • 1 cup cooking oil

Directions


  • Use spring roll wrappers to encase the cheeses.
  • After heating the pan, add the cooking oil. Give the oil a few minutes to warm up.
  • For four minutes, or until the spring roll wrapper is golden brown, deep-fry the cheese inside the wrapper (uncooked cheese sticks).
  • Take out of the pan and transfer to a paper towel-lined plate. To allow the oil to drain, leave it for a few minutes.
  • Serve with ketchup dip and mayonnaise.

Recipe by: Vanjo Merano
