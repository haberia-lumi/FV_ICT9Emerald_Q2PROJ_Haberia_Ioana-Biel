Tapsilog

Ingredients


    Tapa

  • 1lb. beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 3 pieces eggs
  • 6 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 3 tablespoons Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 6 cloves crushed garlic
  • ¾ cups pineapple juice
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon ground white pepper

    • Rice

  • 5 cups leftover rice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed

Directions


  • Combine all the tapa marinade ingredients. Mix well and cover. Place inside the fridge overnight.
  • Heat three tablespoons of cooking oil in a skillet to fry the garlic fried rice. Add the smashed garlic. Sauté the garlic until it becomes lightly browned. Pour in the remaining rice. For three minutes, stir-fry.
  • Add salt for seasoning. Stir-fry for another three to five minutes. Put aside.
  • Get the tapa cooking. Heat a skillet and add the marinated steak and marinade to it. Pour in half a cup of water. Bring the mixture to a boil. Continue cooking with the pan covered until the liquid has reduced by half. To the mixture, add 3 tablespoons of frying oil. Keep cooking until all of the liquid has evaporated. In the remaining oil, fry the beef tapa until it is medium brown. Put aside.
  • Pour one tablespoon of oil into a skillet and fry the egg. Add enough salt to the top of a cracked egg. Cook for 30 seconds. Add two teaspoons of water to the pan's side. Let the water boil with a lid on. Keep cooking until the steam has cooked the egg yolks all the way through.
  • Arrange the beef tapa, sinangag, and fried egg on a large plate to form Tapsilog. Serve with vinegar as dipping sauce for tapa.

Recipe by: Vanjo Merano
