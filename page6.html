Mango Graham

Ingredients


  • 1 tablespoon of crushed graham
  • 20 pieces Graham crackers
  • 1 cup all purpose cream or coolwhip
  • 3/4 cup condensed milk
  • 2 cups of mangoes thinly sliced

Directions


  • Place eight to ten graham crackers in a rectangular dish. Put aside.
  • Combine the condensed milk and cream/cool whip in a bowl.
  • Next, spread the milk and cream mixture on the bottom of the layered crackers.
  • Next, evenly distribute the thinly sliced mangoes over the cream.
  • Spread the cream and mango slices over another layer of graham crackers.
  • Top with crushed graham crackers and a mango garnish.
  • Before serving, let it cool for at least three hours.
